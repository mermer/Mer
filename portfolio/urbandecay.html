#UDHalloween & #UDxGwen

These two campaigns for Urban Decay makeup were developed in Ruby on Rails. My role as front-end developer was to take backend code written by my teammate, Charles Warsh and style it as well as make it fully responsive, as most of their submissions to these contests are through mobile.

#UDHalloween

Urban Decay is known for their edgy makeup looks. Halloween is a perfect time to express that. We created a campaign for their consumers to upload pictures of their best halloween makeup looks. With the help of social media power from their influencers/judges, they received 35,570 visitors and 71,600 page views.

udhalloween.com

#UDxGwen

Urban Decay has a partnership with Gwen Stefani and a new makeup line was launching. We decided to create a campaign for UD that would make people even more excited for this legendary makeup mashup. This campaign does this by having UD's consumers and followers upload a Dubsmash video of themselves singing to one of Gwen Stefani's songs. This fun campaign brought in 35,570 total visitors and 71,600 page views.
