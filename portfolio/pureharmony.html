Pure Harmony is a pet food brand that focuses on natural ingredients. It is directed towards a consumer that would spend more on healthy food for their pets. My role was designing the site under creative director Victoria Deldin. Since the brand focuses on natural ingredients, I included a brown paper texture background on the background of the whole site, as well as using warm colors. I love the areal photos of the ingredients of their food so I definitely wanted to include them. I also developed this responsive Wordpress site using HTML3, CSS3, Javascript, and CSS3 Animations.
