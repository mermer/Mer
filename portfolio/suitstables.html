Suits + Tables

Suits + Tables is a free online service to book events at restaurants. My role in this website is to redesign their current site to make it look fresh and clean. This fresh and clean feel appeals to their target audience, which are mostly people who work in corporate businesses. I used white clean backgrounds and their signature blue as an accent color. I also created fun and colorful icons for each of their venue categories to liven the page up.

Site going live in 2017.
